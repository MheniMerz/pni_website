Platform for Networking Innovation

ANTD is deploying a 100G Platform for Network Innovation (PNI) for measurement research in Network Science, and Engineering. PNI will serve to measure, model, validate, manage, and to verify system performance. Measurement of a complex network is very hard and impossible to carry in a production network. Models change dynamically such as cybersecurity and system resources. How do you know you have a good model from measurement? How do you validate it is still valid? This platform will be the lab environment to conduct this research.

PNI is connected to the Internet2 community which is a consortium of high bandwidth networks connecting multiple universities and research centers. This enables resources and bandwidth extensive research that need to transfer large amounts of data. PNI is deployed in multiple buildings on the NIST Gaithersburrg campus and many NIST researchers are using it to host and run their expermients.
